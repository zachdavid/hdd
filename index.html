Services

How can we help you?

Startup Business Support

Picking the right combination of recreational licensing is critical for a new business's success. We have proven industry experience to help your team pick exactly what's right for your path to success.

Facility Renderings

Maximizing space and operational efficiency is a key component to a successful and profitable business. HDD works with our clients to optimize the space available for cultivations, manufacturing, and retail spaces. We use the latest technology to provide our clients 3D modeling. This enables the client to visualize the optimized space prior to construction.

Building Selection, Architectural Design and Permitting

HDD assists our clients in property selection to ensure proper zoning compliance. Our team can also design and manage any necessary building permitting and tenant improvements.

Financial Services

Allow our team to support your businesses accounting and financial needs. We assist our clients as they set up the financial process infrastructure for long term success.

Advertising and Brand Management

Site Security Plans

Security is important to any business owner as well as the community. HDD works with our clients to develop a plan that ensures all security requirements are in place including but not limited to, security systems, access control, intrusion detections, electrical installation and site surveillance and recording.

Cultivation Schedules

Understanding how to keep your cash flow consistent is an area that has perplexed many newcomers to the industry. Leverage our experience in this realm to keep your customers happy with a steady supply of their favorite products.

Pest Management

Ideally all operations are in a sterile environment with a controlled input of plants, however as we all know life happens and so do frustrating pests that can not only hurt profit margins, but devastate entire crops. HDD offers comprehensive solutions to not only remove predators, but keep them away.
